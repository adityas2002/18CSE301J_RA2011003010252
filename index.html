INDIA-STATICS ANALYSIS

Analysis of Crime Data of different states of India with their GDP, Inflation and Unemployment rates

About Data

  • Crime Data
    Total Cognizable Crime Incidences IPC Crimes and SLL Crimes in India of different states from 2017 to 2022 and Average Cognizable Crime Rate IPC Crimes and SLL Crimes in India of different states from 2017 to 2021.
    GDP Data
    Gross State Domestic Product(Constant Prices) from 2017-2021
    Unemployment
    State-wise Unemployability Rate: Usual Status (Adjusted) (Rural) and (Urban) from 2017-2021
    Inflation
    State-wise Average Inflation(CPI)-General from 2017-2021





DISCLAIMER

There is lack of information about GDP,Inflation,Unemployment or Crime Data of some Union Territories like Ladakh, Daman & Diu Islands,etc and other states. They have been represented as an integral part of India but they may not be coloured (in some visualizations) due to the lack of data. This website considers Ladakh and other uncloured parts which belong to India as an integral part of India.
